## About

Five years building and operating multi-tenant Kubernetes platforms at scale. I focus on three things: **reliability that teams can measure**, **infrastructure that's safe to self-serve**, and **fixing problem classes instead of individual incidents**.

Currently a Senior Platform Engineer at Swisscom. Previously Amdocs and T-Systems.

## Impact

**80% reduction in MTTD** — Designed Prometheus + Grafana observability from scratch for 20+ microservices. Symptom-based alerting with RED dashboards and SLO monitoring. Alert noise down 60%. Mean time to detect dropped from 30 minutes to 6.

**45% fewer pod restarts** — Solved noisy neighbor problems in a multi-tenant K8s platform through namespace-level resource governance. Distributed quotas and limits that hold under real load, not just in tests.

**10x faster deployments** — Led AKS migration for 8 services at Amdocs. Deployment cycle went from 2 hours to 12 minutes. Rebuilt alerting with rate-of-change detection; false positive rate down 80%.

**Fixed a whole problem class, not symptoms** — Analyzed 12 production incidents. 70% shared the same root cause: missing retry logic. Built an exponential backoff library adopted by 8 services. Wrote it once, eliminated a category of incidents.

**Three-team voluntary adoption** — Built Terraform modules (VPC, EKS, RDS) with secure defaults. Teams chose to use them. That's the real signal.
